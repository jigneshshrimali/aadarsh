Our Story

Ten Years. One Standard.

We started as a small web design shop in Rajkot in 2012. A decade later, the team is bigger and the tools have changed — the standard hasn't.

Aadarsh Technologies was founded in Rajkot, Gujarat in 2012, building websites for local businesses who needed a real online presence and couldn't find anyone willing to explain things in plain language.

That's still the core of how we work, even as the services have grown. Web development brought mobile app requests. Mobile apps brought questions about visibility, which brought SEO and digital marketing into the mix. Today we're a full-service digital agency — one team, one point of contact, across web, mobile, search, and content.

We're still based in Rajkot. We still pick up the phone.
